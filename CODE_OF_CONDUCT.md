# 🕊️ Code of Conduct

Champion OS is a project about honest counsel delivered with care: soft on the person, hard on the truth. The community runs the same way.

**Expected:**

- Be direct about the work; be kind to the people doing it. Critique code, personas, and prose as hard as you like, but never the person who wrote them.
- Assume good faith. Most bad PRs are misunderstandings of the one law, not malice.
- Respect privacy reflexively. Never ask anyone to share their dossier, records, or interview answers, and never post someone else's, even in fragments.

**Not tolerated:** harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, sexualized or demeaning conduct, doxxing, or sustained disruption, whether in issues, PRs, discussions, or anywhere the project's name is on the door.

**Enforcement:** report conduct issues privately to the maintainer (see the repository's contact links). Reports are handled confidentially. Consequences scale from a warning to removal from project spaces, at the maintainers' judgment.

The mirror has teeth so people don't have to. Bring the empathy; the prosecutor is already staffed.
