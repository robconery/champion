# Marketing Playbook, the flywheel

One page. The whole system. If a marketing idea doesn't fit on this page, it's probably a scheme, not a strategy.

## The strategy in one line

Teach a real outcome in public, trade it for an email, keep teaching, invite softly and often. The content is the product demo. Nobody gets sold. They get helped, then they raise their hand.

## The flywheel

| Stage | The job | Tool | The one metric |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Attract | Teach a real outcome in public | Video (every other week) + newsletter | New reach / subscribers per piece |
| 2. Capture | One lead magnet: the exact thing the content demoed | Email form → the asset | Opt-in rate |
| 3. Nurture | Deliver the asset, keep teaching, standing invite | Email sequence + community workflows | Click-through to the offer |
| 4. Convert | Free taste → membership → cohort | Community spaces + paywall + payments | Free→paid conversion |
| 5. Compound | Members post wins → wins become content | Community posts, events, segments | Wins posted / referrals |

## The three disciplines

1. **One engine, one asset, one offer.** Engine = video. Asset = the email list. Offer = the user's one paid offer. Everything points one direction. No funnels inside funnels. (Graham)
2. **The build is the marketing.** Each video teaches a real system, and the lead magnet is the exact thing you demoed. People download the outcome, not a promise. (Nate)
3. **Value over scheme.** When stuck, make the free thing more valuable. Never make the scheme more clever.

## The asset stack

- **Content:** video + newsletter (the engine). Source of truth for pieces: `~/.champion/work/content/`.
- **Lead magnet:** the one asset traded for an email, the exact thing the content demos. See `~/.champion/work/lead-magnet/`.
- **Community + paywall:** the user's community platform.
- **Money:** the payment processor (source of truth for revenue).
- **Email + automation:** the user's email platform. If the community platform can hold and email the broader, non-member list well, consolidating on one system is usually the right call.

## Cadence (the rhythm that beats intensity)

- **Every other week:** ship one video. Non-negotiable. This is the flywheel's turn.
- **Weekly:** one newsletter that teaches and links the lead magnet.
- **Always-on:** the nurture sequence runs automatically for every new subscriber.
- **Per cohort:** a short, simple open → close window. No chaotic launches.
